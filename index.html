Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

VibeMemory ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

Data Collection

VibeMemory is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties.

Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create in VibeMemory is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Memories: Titles, dates, notes, ratings, and category details you enter.
  • Photos: Images you select from your photo library or capture with your camera are stored within the app's local database.
  • Location data: The coordinates and address of memories you create, based on locations you select on the map.
  • Preferences: Your chosen theme and language settings.

Permissions

VibeMemory requests the following permissions:

  • Location (When In Use): Used to show your current position on the map and to associate a location with your memories. Your location is never sent to any server.
  • Photo Library: Used to let you select photos to attach to your memories. Photos are stored locally and never uploaded anywhere.
  • Camera: Used to let you take photos directly for your memories. Photos are stored locally and never uploaded anywhere.

Third-Party Services

VibeMemory does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not integrate any SDKs that collect user data.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party. Your memories stay on your device.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device for as long as you use the app. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

VibeMemory does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require any account creation or personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
